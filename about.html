About St Andrews Park

Our Green Space

St Andrews Park is a cherished green space in the heart of Norwich, established in 1923. Spanning over 12 acres, the park features beautiful gardens, playgrounds, sports facilities, and peaceful walking paths.

The park is maintained through a partnership between Norwich City Council and the Friends of St Andrews Park volunteer group.

History of the Park

The land was originally part of the St Andrews Hall estate before being donated to the city for public use. The park was formally opened in 1923 and has been a community hub ever since.

In 2015, the park underwent a major renovation funded by the Heritage Lottery Fund, restoring many original features while adding modern amenities.
