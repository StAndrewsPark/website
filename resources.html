Frequently Asked Questions

Yes, there are public toilets located near the main entrance. They are open from 8am until dusk. There is also an accessible toilet with RADAR key access.

There is limited street parking around the park. The closest public car park is St Andrews Car Park on Duke Street (NR3 3AT), about a 5-minute walk away.

Cycling is permitted only on the main tarmac paths and must be done with consideration for pedestrians. No cycling is allowed in play areas or on grass areas.

We welcome volunteers! You can join our monthly park cleanups, help with gardening, or assist at events. Contact the Friends group at friends@standrewsparknorwich.org
